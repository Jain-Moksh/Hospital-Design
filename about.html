About TitansHealthCare


Situated in South Mumbai is the TitansHealthCare Hospital and Medical Research Centre, the medical hub of India's biggest metropolis. Established over seven decades ago, in 1952, The TitansHealthCare Hospital was the result of the enormous philanthropy displayed by Shri Rameshwardas Birla, Founder Chairman of the TitansHealthCare Hospital Trust.


Achievements of TitansHealthCare


Shri Rameshwardas Birla, Founder Chairman of the TitansHealthCare Hospital Trust has been awarded padmashri award by our respected president Droupadi Murmu.

TitansHealthCare is awarded as the number one Hospitalin terms of care and support as well as treatement by Hindustan Times and times of India.
